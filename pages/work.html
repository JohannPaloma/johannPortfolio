Felices Fiestas

This was my first approach to Autodesk Maya. As my thesis I decided to write a shortfilm which only could be done through animation. There were almost ten versions of the screenplay and three of them got animated; the first as a trial run to see if I could do it, the second as a experiment of different techniques recently learned, and the third as the final result of two years of writing, learning and exploring.
